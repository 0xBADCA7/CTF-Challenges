Our Universe has a small alien race, Crypto Chickens. Their planet is famous with arsenal, protected by very crypto-strong system, that can turn the tide of the war between Earthlings and Aliens in our direction! Backroom interplanetary exploration has learned that race Crypto Chickens includes 1,000 individuals, and each of them has a key. It is only when all keys are connected together, you can open the capsule with an arsenal. For 10 years, our agents have engaged in theft of keys of the race Crypto Chickens. Finally, they all turned out, they dragged all the keys and the capsule into the bargain. But on the way to Earth they were overtaken by the chase and all cryptographers able to open the capsule, were killed. Help to open the capsule and win this war.
